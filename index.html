MovieHive App Policies

1. Purpose of the App

MovieHive is an app designed to help users discover movies and TV shows. It provides information such as descriptions, ratings, cast, genres, country of origin, and airing details. The app does not host or stream movies/TV shows. It only displays publicly available metadata.

2. Data Sources

All movie and TV show data shown in MovieHive comes from trusted third-party APIs:

  • TVMaze
  • The Movie Database (TMDb)
